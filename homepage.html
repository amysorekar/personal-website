    March 2019 - May 2019

    The Story!

    Smoothie King was my first ever job. It was my first foray at applying for jobs, asking for references, and eagerly waiting for results.

    I remember asking my math teacher and my cross country coach for a reference. My only resume content was the classes I had taken and my extra-curriculars.

    I had applied to many restaurants as a dishwasher, busboy, any menial job I could possibly get. I just wanted to start working!

    *DING!* A notification! I had gotten a message from the manager of Smoothie King. He wanted to interview me! I was overjoyed. My first shot at real working experience!

    On the day of the interview, I spiffied up and headed over. Steve, the manager, asked me some basic questions about why I wanted to work there. A few minutes later and he was shaking my hand and congratulating me.

    My career was short-lived, however. Randomly, I started getting assigned less and less hours. I was a fine worker, and I got along well with my coworkers. I tried calling and texting Steve. No response. I tried emailing him. Same thing. I tried to go in person several times, but he was always traveling.
